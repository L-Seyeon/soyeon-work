Artist / Painter

Soyeon C

Meet Soyeon, a painter known for her explosive color and layered gestures. With a passion for the boundary between chaos and order, she transforms raw energy into canvases that breathe. Her work reflects a deep connection to nature, emotion, and the subconscious — inviting viewers to feel what cannot be spoken.

Her philosophy revolves around the concept of finding vitality in the untamed — capturing the essence of fleeting impulse and raw sensation. She believes that every stroke, no matter how spontaneous, has the potential to tell a unique story and evoke emotion. Through her paintings, Soyeon seeks to immortalize these moments of pure expression, preserving them in layers of color and inviting viewers to pause, reflect, and find meaning in the beauty of abstraction. Her work is a celebration of the energy of nature and the poetry of unrestrained existence.
